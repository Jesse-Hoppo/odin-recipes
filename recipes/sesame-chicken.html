Sesame Chicken

A beautifully cooked Sesame Chicken

The BEST Sesame Chicken in Australia. Beautifully cooked and finished to satisfy even the pickiest of eaters.

The meal consists of pieces or strips of chicken stir-fried in an oyster sauce with ginger and garlic till cooked through. Green onions, sliced, are also used to round off this delicious supper. Some versions of Sesame Chicken come with rice or noodles, while others utilize carrots instead of chicken as the main element.

Ingredients

-- Chicken --

  • 5 tbsp Vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbsp cornflour (cornstrach)
  • 10 tbsp plain (all-purpose) flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic salt
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 3 chicken breast fillets

-- Sauce --

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp chinese rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
  • 3 tbsp kethcup
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce

-- To serve --

  • Boiled rice
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • Small bunch spring onions/scallions

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a wok or large frying pan until very hot
  2. Whilst oil is heating, place egg in one shallow bowl, the cornflour in another shallow ball. Add the flour, salt, pepper, garlic salt and paprika to another bown and mix together
  3. Dredge the chicken in the cornflour, then dip in the egg ( Make sure all of the chicken is covered in the egg wash)
  4. Add to the wok and cook on high heat for 6-7 minutes, turning two or three times during cooking until golden brown. Remove from pan when cooked and place in a bowl lined with kicthen towel.
  5. Add all of the sauce ingredients to the hot wok, stir and bubble on high heat until sauce redcued to a third (2-3 minutes)
  6. Add th chicken back in and toss in the sauce until well coated. Cook for a further 1-2 minutes.
  7. Divide between 4 bowls and serve with boiled rice. Top with spring onions and sesame seeds.
