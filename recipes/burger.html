A beautifully cooked Burger

The MOST mouth-watering burger you'll ever make

Ingredients

Burger

  • 500g beef mince
  • 1/2 small brown onion, coarsely grated
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 2 tbsp Chipotle bbq sauce, extra to serve
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped fresh oregano
  • 45g dried or packeged breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg, lightly wgisked
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 4 large slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 large brioche olls, halved and toasted
  • 8 rashes premium buther style bacon
  • 85g (1/3 cup) tomato relish
  • baby rocket leaves

Steps

  1. Place the mince, onion, garlic, chipotle bbq sauce, oregno, breadcrumbs and egg in a bowl. Season. Use clean hands to mix until well combined. Divide into 4 equal portions and shape into 1cm thick pattys
  2. Heat oil in large frying pan over medium heat. Cook the patties for 5 minutes. Turn the patties and top with a peice of the cheese. Cook for 5 minutes or until cooked throigh and cheese is melted. Transfer to a plate.
  3. Add brioche buns to pan, cute side down and cook for 2 minutes or until lightly toasted. Set aside
  4. girll half the bacon for 3 minutes each side or until crisp, tranfer to plate lined with paper towl to cover and keep warm, repeat with remaining bacon.
  5. Spread the bases of the buns with tomato relish. Top with rocket, patties and bacon. Drizzles extra chipotle sauce and top with the top brioche bun.
